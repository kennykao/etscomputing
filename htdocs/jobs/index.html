Our Mission

Educational Technology Services enhances and supports the UC Berkeley academic experience by providing essential, responsive, and innovative technology services. ETS strives to be a responsive service organization with a clearly defined and aligned service portfolio. We strive to create collaboration points across campus and across higher education to improve user experience and better support teaching and learning!

Instructional Computer Facilities provide an easy location for instructors and campus groups who need a space with up-to-date computers/software to lead instruction or group collaborations.

Drop-in Computer Facilities provide a space available to students, staff, and instructors to access computers to fit their academic and work needs. Locations of our facilities are conveniently located across campus with extended hours to fit your schedule!

Currently Hiring

Student Consultant

Payroll Title: Assistant I
Reports To: ETS-Supervisors
Pay Rate: $12.00/hour
Hours: 10-16 per week

Interested in working at an on campus job that supports both your interpersonal and professional growth while being respectful of your academic priorities? Look no further! The ETS Student Consultant serves as the front-line support to the Berkeley campus general access and instructional facilities. Through this position you will assist clients in troubleshooting and using computer software such as Adobe Creative Suite and Microsoft Office while fostering a productive lab environment to maximize customer satisfaction.

This position presents the unique opportunity to work in a community that is intent on providing a supportive work environment. There are also opportunities to join a skilled team (which includes hours that pay at $15.11) focused on specialized projects such as Marketing, Design, Knowledge Management, Training, and more!

Minimum Requirements:

  • UC Berkeley Student with Work Study
  • Basic understanding of Mac and PC computers and their respective operating systems
  • Basic knowledge in common application, networking, and email software such as Microsoft Office, Google Drive, Chrome, and Firefox

Auditorium Staff

Payroll Title: Assistant I
Reports To: ETS-Supervisors
Pay Rate: $12.00/hour
Hours: 10-16 per week

Interested in working at an on campus job that supports both your interpersonal and professional growth while being respectful of your academic priorities? Look no further! The ETS Auditorium Staff position presents an opportunity to be directly involved with an integral operation of UC Berkeley's educational functions.

The Auditorium Staff position serves to set up and monitor the Audio and Visual equipment in the auditorium before and during lectures. Through this position you will operate the camera monitoring, switching, and cutting devices in order to enhance the experience of both professors and students.

Minimum Requirements:

  • UC Berkeley Student with Work Study
  • Professionalism and clear communication while interacting with professors and faculty
  • Basic experience in troubleshooting and resolving software issues

Junior Analyst - HelpDesk for Online Services (HDOS)

Payroll Title: Assistant III
Reports To: ETS-Supervisors
Pay Rate: $15.11/hour
Hours: 8-12 per week

Interested in providing front-line support for online services supported by the ETS Help Desk for Online Services? Look no further! The ETS Junior Analyst position provides quality customer service and technical support to client with 90% of efforts being put into the Learning Management system, bCourses.

The position also serves to respond to ticket inquiries through a ticketing system and to provide phone and in-person support for bCourses.

Minimum Requirements:

  • UC Berkeley Student with Work Study
  • Ability to multitask between calls, tickets, and in-person demands
  • Friendly demeanor to clients
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills
  • Strong customer service, organizational, and analytical skills

HelpDesk Dispatch Analyst (HDDA)

Payroll Title: Assistant II
Reports To: ETS-Supervisors
Pay Rate: $14.00/hour
Hours: 8-12 per week

Interested in providing front line phone support for onsite services? Join the Dispatch team! This position has two roles, one requires troubleshooting issues with the AV equipment in classrooms over the phone, and the other entails interacting with ETS staff and partners at the front desk in the Academic Innovation Studio.

These roles require excellent customer service skills both over the phone and in person. You will be responsible for assisting instructors with classroom technology support issues and then dispatching student consultants. You will also interact frequently with professional ETS staff members, so an outgoing and professional personality is a must.

Minimum Requirements:

  • UC Berkeley Student with Work Study
  • Ability to multitask between calls, tickets, and in-person demands
  • Ability to communicate clearly and calmly while assisting with Classroom Technology Support
  • Basic familiarity of/ willingness to learn about AV classroom equipment
